Empowering Lives Through Innovation in Oncology

I Am Still Alive LLC, is a global initiative dedicated to transforming oncology and radiotherapy services. More than just a cancer survivorship platform, we are building a comprehensive ecosystem that extends to, other critical healthcare needs. I am Still Alive bridge the gap between patients, healthcare providers, and industry leaders, by creating innovative solutions that enhance accessibility, affordability, and quality of care.

Our Mission

We are committed to establishing an integrated, patient-centric platform for oncology care, with a strong foundation in radiotherapy and functional radiosurgery. By leveraging technology, strategic partnerships, and patient-driven innovation, we aim to elevate global cancer care standards and foster meaningful connections between patients, medical professionals, and the corporate sector.

Our Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize cancer treatment and support services by:

  • Collaborating with academia, healthcare providers, and industry leaders.
  • Developing specialized services for veterans, first responders, and underserved populations.
